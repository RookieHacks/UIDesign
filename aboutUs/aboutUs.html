About Us
ShareCare is a platform where users can learn new info related to COVID-19 and other medical fields by taking small quizzes for which
the information is provided by renowned medical institutions from across the globe.
By completing a quiz, you will then earn points for which you can spend on gift cards and other premium rewards. Furthermore,
for every quiz you take, we will donate $1 each time to hospitals! Its a win - win scenario! To add on, the more points you
obtain, the higher you will go on the leaderboard. The leaderboard is a place where you and your friends can
engage in friendly competition to see who can learn the most! Another feature of ShareCare is that you can
directly donate, using your money, to your favourite hospitals without the need of taking quizzes!

Our website will be funded through advertisements so we can continue to donate!

Now go and learn something new!

We hope you will enjoy!
